BMGT 407 - INFORMATION SYSTEM PROJECT
BMGT 407 is the capstone course for Information Systems (IS) majors, where students apply their knowledge to develop real-world IS solutions for business clients. Through a complete IS development process, students design and implement functional prototypes that solve practical business challenges, preparing them for careers in technology and consulting.
